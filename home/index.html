Who we are

Codezilla is a community for those who see things from a different perspective - the Contributors. We are a Mozilla verified Community and a Campus Club of SRM Ramapuram that provides a platform for community, code, and collaboration.

What we do

Codezilla has a place for all who strive to learn and work towards a better world. Our community members gain:

  • Exposure to cutting edge tech via opensource
  • Preparation for the professional world by rising to meet real-time challenges
  • Enhanced soft skills through collaboration
